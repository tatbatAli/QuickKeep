We only collect what you choose to sync

Free users save everything locally — no account, no server, nothing leaves your device. If you upgrade to Pro or Team, your saves sync to Firebase (by Google) so you can access them across devices. We never read, sell, or share what you save.

No tracking, ever

There are no analytics, cookies, or tracking scripts inside the extension. We don't track what you browse, how often you use the app, or anything about your behavior. Your account email is used only for login — nothing else.

Permissions we ask for

QuickKeep needs permission to read the current tab's URL and title when you save, inject the quick-save overlay on pages, and store your saves locally or sync them to Firebase if you're on a paid plan. We never read page content unless you highlight text to save as a note.

You're in full control

Delete any save, clear your entire list, or cancel your plan anytime. Free users: uninstalling removes everything permanently. Pro/Team users: your cloud data stays in Firebase until you delete your account. Your personal saves are always private — team members can only see what you explicitly share to the team.

Third-party services we use

Paddle — Payments are processed by Paddle (our Merchant of Record). When you upgrade, your payment info is collected directly by Paddle. We never see your card details. Paddle Privacy Policy →

Firebase (by Google) — We use Firebase for cloud sync, database storage, and authentication for Pro and Team users. Firebase processes your email and saved content on our behalf to deliver the service. Firebase Privacy Policy →

Questions?

Reach out via the Chrome Web Store support link. We're a small team and always happy to help — whether it's a billing question, a privacy concern, or just feedback about the product.

Your Data Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data — saved URLs, notes, and profile information — at any time directly from the extension. Open the extension, click your profile icon, and select "Delete Account." Once deleted, your data is removed from our active databases permanently. We may retain certain transaction records for legal and tax purposes as required by Paddle.
