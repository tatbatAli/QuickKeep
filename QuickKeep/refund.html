Refund Policy

Last updated: February 2026  ·  Applies to all paid plans

1
How Billing Works

QuickKeep offers paid subscriptions on a monthly or yearly basis, processed securely through Paddle, our payment provider. Paddle is the merchant of record for all QuickKeep transactions, which means they handle all payment processing, receipts, and refund transactions on our behalf.

When you subscribe to a Pro or Group plan, your card is charged immediately and your plan is activated right away. You will receive a receipt by email from Paddle after every charge. Subscriptions renew automatically at the end of each billing period — monthly plans renew every 30 days, yearly plans renew every 365 days. You will always be notified before a renewal charge is made.

2
Free Trial

Every new QuickKeep account receives a 7-day free Pro trial with no credit card required. During the trial you have full access to all Pro features including unlimited saves, cross-device sync, instant search, and export. The trial exists so you can experience the full product before deciding to subscribe.

At the end of the 7-day trial your account automatically reverts to the free plan. You will not be charged anything unless you actively choose to subscribe to a paid plan.

No card is required for the free trial. You will never be charged without explicitly choosing a paid plan and entering your payment details.
3
Cancellation and Access

You may cancel your subscription at any time directly from the extension by opening your account menu and selecting Manage Subscription. This takes you to the Paddle customer portal where you can cancel with no friction.

When you cancel, your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period. For example, if you are on a monthly plan and cancel on the 15th of the month, you keep full access to all paid features until the end of that month. No further charges are made after cancellation. Your saved data is never deleted — it remains accessible on the free plan.

4
Group Plan Cancellation

If you are the owner of a Group plan and you cancel, all members of your group are downgraded to the free plan at the end of the billing period. Members will lose access to shared group features — the shared saves tab, live activity feed, and reactions — but their personal saves are never affected. Everything they saved privately remains fully intact.

Members who were added to a group are never charged. Only the group owner pays. If the owner cancels, members simply return to their free accounts automatically.

5
Refunds

QuickKeep offers paid subscriptions on a monthly or yearly basis, processed securely through Paddle, our payment provider. When you subscribe to a Pro or Group plan, your card is charged immediately and your plan is activated right away. Subscriptions renew automatically at the end of each billing period — monthly plans renew every 30 days, yearly plans renew every 365 days. You will receive a receipt by email from Paddle after each charge.

When you cancel a subscription, your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period. For example, if you are on a monthly plan and cancel on the 15th of the month, you keep full access to your paid features until the end of that month. No further charges are made after cancellation. You can cancel at any time directly from the extension by clicking your account menu and selecting Manage Subscription.

If you are on a Group plan and you cancel, all members of your group are downgraded to the free plan at the end of the billing period. Their personal saves are never affected — only the shared group features are removed.

If you have any issue with a charge or simply change your mind, you may request a full refund within 14 days of the charge by emailing support@quickkeep.icu with the email address on your account. We process all refund requests within 48 hours.

6
Contact

For any billing questions, refund requests, or payment issues, reach us at support@quickkeep.icu. We respond to all inquiries within 48 hours. You can also reach us via the support link on our Chrome Web Store listing.
